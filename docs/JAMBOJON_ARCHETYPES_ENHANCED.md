# Jambojon Archetypes - Enhanced Framework
## Comprehensive Descriptions, Synonyms, and Theme Identification

**Framework:** Brand & Narrative Archetypes  
**Version:** 2.0 - Enhanced with Comprehensive Keywords  
**Total Archetypes:** 12 (Standard Jungian) + Narrative Patterns  
**Scoring:** 0.0 to 1.0 fractional (NO WEIGHTS - all archetypes equal)

---

## Scoring Philosophy

**Every archetype counts equally.** No arbitrary weights. Simple averages throughout.

- Each archetype: Individual score 0.0-1.0
- Overall score: Simple average of all 12 archetype scores
- Identify dominant archetypes (top 3-5)
- Identify supporting archetypes
- Identify missing/weak archetypes

---

## Scoring Scale

- **0.8-1.0** - **Dominant Archetype** - Strongly present, core identity
- **0.6-0.79** - **Supporting Archetype** - Present and influential
- **0.4-0.59** - **Moderate Presence** - Occasional use, secondary theme
- **0.0-0.39** - **Weak/Absent** - Rarely used or not present
